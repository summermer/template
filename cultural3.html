Analysis of Artifact

Tea ceremony is also considered as a way of cultivating the mind, become an intimate with nature, and evaluating us through self-reflection. There is strong connection between tea and Buddhism in China. Firstly, the relationship between tea and Buddhist monks is that tea provides irreplaceable drink for monks, and then monks are in a great help of promoting the development of tea production and technical progress. Therefore, practically, the Buddhism and tea ceremony found more and more ideological connotation in common, which is stated as a proverb says: “Taste Zen in tea.” Zen is literally overwhelming and too general, but it can be separated into four big themes, which could be expressed by tea: bitter, quiet, ordinary, and release.
